## Deliverables
- [ ] EU AI Act compliance validation
  - Article 12(2)(a): Right to explanation - verify all decisions have explanations
  - Article 18: High-risk AI documentation - verify checkpoints and audit trails
  - Article 19: Human oversight - verify escalation and review capabilities
  - Article 26(6): Documentation - verify all required docs present
  - Generate compliance certificate
- [ ] GDPR compliance validation
  - Data processing audit - verify all processing logged
  - Data subject rights - verify erasure, access capabilities
  - Data retention - verify policies enforced
  - DPA requirements - verify documentation complete
- [ ] SOC2 compliance validation
  - Security controls - verify access controls, monitoring, alerting
  - Availability - verify uptime, redundancy, failover
  - Confidentiality - verify encryption, key management
  - Integrity - verify audit logs, tamper detection
- [ ] Additional regulatory frameworks
  - ISO/IEC 24970 (AI Governance) - verify alignment
  - Industry-specific regs (HIPAA, etc.) if applicable
- [ ] Compliance validation report
  - Summary: compliant or non-compliant
  - Evidence: specific findings and proof
  - Gaps: any identified non-compliance items
  - Remediation: plan for any gaps

## Acceptance Criteria
- [ ] EU AI Act Articles 12, 18, 19, 26(6) validated
- [ ] GDPR validated
- [ ] SOC2 validated
- [ ] Additional frameworks validated
- [ ] Compliance report completed
